What's this?

The recruitment leaderboard tracks Welcomers' Guild member's recruitment ranking points (WGP).
The first number is the nation's current WGP.
The second number is the nation's net monthly WGP, with the bonus WGP included. Hover over it to view the WGP gained without bonuses.
The third number is the nation's WGP Daily Rate.
For more information, view the FAQ.
