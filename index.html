What's this?

The recruitment leaderboard tracks Welcomer's Guild member's recruitment ranking points (RRP).
The first number is the nation's current RRP.
The second number (in green, red or grey) is the nation's net monthly RRP, with the bonus RRP included. Hover over it to view the base RRP gain.
For more information, view the FAQ.
